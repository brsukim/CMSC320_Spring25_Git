Answer: Because version control keeps things organized.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: It can be used in journalism. There are many datasets that contain stories that we do not know until data scientists parse and analyse them.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the commit history by creating a new merge commit, keeping the branch structure intact. Rebasing rewrites history by applying commits from one branch onto another sequentially, creating a cleaner, linear history.
